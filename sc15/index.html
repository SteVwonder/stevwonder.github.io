About Flux

Flux is an extensible job and resource management framework specifically designed to deal with the requirements of next-generation HPC centers. Flux targets an entire computing facility as one common pool of diverse sets of resources, enabling the facility to accommodate site-wide constraints (e.g., for power limits). Yet, its scalable and distributed design still offers scalable and effective scheduling strategies.
